# Trading Research Evidence

Required output sections:

- Objective
- Data window and source
- Method
- Metrics
- Out-of-sample or validation approach when available
- Assumptions
- Limitations
- Confidence
- Recommendation input for Strategist, not final decision

If data, tooling, or capability is missing, stop and draft a capability-gap handoff instead of fabricating results.
